Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve a room?

Find a house in the 'Search' or 'Map' tab and click the '+' button to send a request.

Where is my reservation status?

Check the 'Activity' tab to see if your request is pending, approved, or declined.

How to update my details?

Go to your 'Profile' tab to change your home address and contact information.
